Turkey Bangla Calligraphy Exibition

Last year, a piece of my work was selected for an international calligraphy exibition in Dhaka, Bangladesh. The exibition was organized by Movencare in association with Turkish embassy.



Co-Author of a University book

I have written a chapter about Bangla Ambigram in a reference book for the Institute of Fine Arts, Dhaka University. This book is about subcontinental Bangla calligraphy and it is yet to publish.
